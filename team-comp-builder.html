    How this scoring works

    Each of the five specs contributes a tier value (S=4, A=3, B=2, C=1) based on its current Mythic+ ranking. The average becomes your letter grade. Required utilities (Bloodlust, Battle Rez, Mass Dispel) penalize the score when missing — because they matter more than raw DPS at high keys.
    Bloodlust (Hero / Time Warp / Primal Rage / Fury of the Aspects) gives the entire party 30% haste for 40 seconds, used twice per dungeon. Without it, you're roughly 15% slower overall — meaning more boss enrages, more affix complications, and longer keys. Bring a Mage, Shaman, Hunter, or Evoker, or use a Drums of Fury consumable.
    In M+ keys at +12 and below, yes — most groups can recover from a death without it. At higher keys, a missing Battle Rez often ends the run. Druid, Death Knight, Warlock, and Beast Mastery Hunter all provide one. We flag this as a warning rather than a hard penalty.
